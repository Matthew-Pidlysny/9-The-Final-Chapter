============
REGAL BODIES
============

== Federal Works Department ==

Intended to manage federal acts and regulations, among other general duties.

== Biodiversity Symposium ==

A body of individuals working the wisdom of biodiversity and assigning to each planet new species, at the right time.

== House of the People's Acts ==

Meant to represent the people in their acts of vocal outcry for or against the state, for any legitimate reason.

== The Giants Organization ==

Meant to organize a few select individuals to keep up to date with the proceedings involving the acts of Giants among their people.

== Office of The Protectorate ==

Combining military, intelligence and diplomatic arms, meant to protect the people from threats within the Intergalactic Federation

== Office of Dark Punishments ==

When a dark punishment is considered against a living being within the Intergalactic Federation by the Black Lavel, the resolution of the standards for that dark punishment will be completed between the Black Lavel and this office.

== Intercontinental Affairs Department ==

For those entering the Dark Continent, this body will administrate the proceedings set forth by the King on the matter.

== Interspecies Challenge & Affairs Department ==

Separate from the Federal Works Department, and only intended to manage the attribues of both things depicted in the title.

== Office of the Intergalactic Judiciary ==

Setting up a separate court for the Intergalactic Federation's use, and managing the nature of legal proceedings within it.

== Federal Monetary Program ==

Regulating the use of currency on all the worlds, including the manner of Shimarra on lands not regulated by the Black Lavel

== Office of Sage Wisdom ==

Meant for sharing the duties of the King with the Chamber of Sages, and incorporating their wisdom on matters more readily.

== House of Lords ==

Given that some planets and systems will serve a lord, such as Earth, before the King's eminence, this body will join them together and generate wisdom.

== The Mekinde ==

A set of voices from the dark land, discussing various topics, for the sake of the King's learning of the nature of Dark Eminence.  It also generates calls to action for the King.

================
ASSOCIATE BODIES
================

== Dark Outreach Office ==

Meant to provide outreach on behalf of Allah to the individuals living under the rule of the Black Lavel.

== The Publisher ==

Intended to provide the publication on internal wisdom ringing out in every single lifeform in existence, from matters light to dark.

== Celebratory Affairs Department ==

When a celebration is intended to be presented to the people in honor of a great wisdom, this body will represent and act for and with the Sages.

== Kramitose Luki ==

Meaning "Law Wisdom Body" in the Dark Language, this one will act to present the legacy of wisdom inherent in following certain legal practices.

== The Jennevie Group ==

To promote the advancement of medicine on various planets, this associate body will work with the people to develop rational and new practices to adopt for their own good.

== Shelter Administration ==

Due to constitutional obligation, each state will provide for the full housing needs of each individual, and this body will help states with whatever they can offer to ease the process where difficult.

== Angelic Outreach Bureau ==

This body is in place to promote the activity of angelic beings in society, to better accomodate those who believe firmly in certain religions where this would benefit the congregation.

== Council of Anarchy and Self Lordship ==

By request of the Angels, this body was developed to help promote individuals trapped within the paradigm of anarchy from the state, whenever it occurs, whether permanent or temporary.
