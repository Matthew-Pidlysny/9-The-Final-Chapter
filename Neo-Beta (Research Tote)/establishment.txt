P(1) = ~5.9127

So it takes +8 to get to 13, and conversely this becomes a negative to say you subtract to get there.  Going along the Beta sequence (4 - 9 = -5, and so on), study this for ANY integer or decimal increase in x, find the residues, establish the Beta sequence.
