## VERIFICATION METHODOLOGY

### Cross-Reference Sources Used:
1. **Reuters Institute Digital News Report 2024**
2. **World Press Freedom Index 2024**
3. **Reporters Without Borders**
4. **Editor & Publisher International Yearbook**
5. **Wan-Ifra World Press Trends**
6. **Alliance for Audited Media Circulation Reports**
7. **SimilarWeb Traffic Analytics**
8. **Alexa Rankings**
9. **Media Bias/Fact Check Database**
10. **Pew Research Center Journalism Studies**

### Verification Criteria:
#### Operational Status:
- **Active Website**: Live domain with regular updates
- **Social Media Presence**: Active Twitter/Facebook accounts
- **Content Freshness**: Articles published within last 24 hours
- **Server Response**: <3 second loading time
- **Mobile Optimization**: Responsive design verified

#### Credibility Assessment:
- **Editorial Standards**: Published ethics guidelines
- **Correction Policy**: Clear error correction process
- **Source Diversity**: Multiple bylines and perspectives
- **Fact-Checking**: Internal verification procedures
- **Transparency**: Ownership and funding disclosed

#### Influence Measurement:
- **Circulation Numbers**: Print/digital subscribers
- **Web Traffic**: Monthly unique visitors
- **Social Engagement**: Shares, likes, comments
- **Media Citations**: References by other outlets
- **Political Impact**: Policy/influence indicators
